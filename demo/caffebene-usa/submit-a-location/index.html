Caffebene occupies more commercial real estate than any other tenant in the world and we're still growing.

Non-Traditional Locations

With our flexible floor plans, almost any site could become a Caffebene. We are ready to fit in any non-traditional sites worldwide, including colleges, airports, hospitals, convenience stores, cinemas, hotels, zoos, casinos, museums, amusement parks and sports arenas.

What is Caffebene Real Estate?

Caffebene Inc, has an exclusive contract with the franchisor of the Caffebene franchise concept to negotiate leases for franchisees. Landlords and franchisees reap the benefits of brand-name recognition, a proven track record and a complete support system.

What We’re Looking For

Caffebene Inc. is interested in securing prime locations in your area right now! The Caffebene chain is expanding, and there is high demand for suitable sites worldwide.

Caffebene Leasing Process

All real estate selections for Caffebene begin with a proposal to a local Development Agent. To have information forwarded to a DA office in your area, submit a location online. The initial draft of the lease for desired locations is sent to a representative of Caffebene Inc. for review. Caffe Bene negotiates provisions with the landlord and arrives at a lease that meets both the landlord’s and the tenant’s needs.
